Hi! My name is Nhi Doan. I am a graphic design student at San Francisco State University. I am good at working with both pixel and vector graphics. I create designs for branding, prints, and web interface, like this one. My domain for user interface and user experience is mainly for older adults to help them feel more comfortable when using apps. I am flexible and easy to work with. I love to observe and create beautiful and interesting designs. Even though doodling and sketching is the way to keep me creating creative stuff, taking a long walk around the city or in the park is my favorite way to clear my mind and observe things in nature. I love to spend time in the sun. My recent hobby is recording footage of wherever I go in San Francisco and sharing it with others.
