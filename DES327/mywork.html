PROJECTS

Wakee Black Coffee
Wakee Coffee is designed for students at SFSU who are busy working and studying on campus most of their time. The drink is to energize the students to start a fresh new day at school and help them stay awake late at night for studying. Wakee Coffee offers black coffee that not only helps the student to stay energetic but also maintains their healthy lifestyle on campus.
Pop-up Card Project
Pop-up card for friends and families during the Christmas holidays. The pop-up card is featured different kinds of pop-up mechanisms. When opening the card, the Christmas tree will stand up with a surprised gift-box opening out. There are also pockets on the card for lucky money.
